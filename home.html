Welcome to ClearRead

A place where you can read your files with ease.

ClearRead to is a web application where users who struggle with long or short-sightedness are able to read their documents with ease!
How it works:
1.Upload a txt file of your choice after you press the get started button below.
2. The contents of your file will be displayed in the box below.
3. Press the 'Magnify' button above your document to access the magnifier.
4. Read and edit your document contents.
5. Enjoy reading!
